# Product

**BadgerGym** is a simple mobile-first gym management web app (PWA).

## What it does

- Track gym **members** with membership plans (monthly, quarterly, annual) and expiry dates
- Schedule and view **classes** by day of week with instructor and capacity info
- Log member **check-ins** and view them on the dashboard
- Record **payments** and see monthly revenue at a glance

## Primary users

Small gym owners and staff managing day-to-day operations from a phone or tablet.

## Key value propositions

- No install required — runs in any browser, installable as a PWA
- All data stored locally in the browser (localStorage)
- Clean, dark mobile UI optimised for touch
